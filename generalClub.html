General Club

The general Club inculcates Indian Culture and Heritage by conducting various activities.

International Friendship Day

The general Club organized Friendship Day celebrations. The day was filled with laughter, joy, excitement and enthusiasm. Competitions like friendship band making and a group ‘Selfie’ contest was organized on the account of Friendship Day.

International Literacy Day

International Literacy Day was organized by the colleges. On this occasion, a seminar titled “Significance of International Literacy Day” was organized.

National Library Week

Indian Library Association declared 14th November as National Library day since 1968. 14th to 20th November has been celebrated as National Library week all over India and various programs are organized to let the public know about the libraries. The purpose of National Library Week is to promote the use and support of Libraries. It is also a time to celebrate the contributions made by Libraries and Librarians in India.

To encourage the students and to promote the use of library, Library week was observed On this occasion Department of Library and Information Centre conducted the several competitions for MBA/MCA/Integrated MBA students. As a part of the week a book exhibition was also organized where several book stalls were displayed. They had also awarded the best users among students and faculty.

The following activities are conducted on the occasion of National Library Week

Essay writing competition: The Essay writing competition held on 15th November, 2017 on the topic of “Digital Payments”.

Debate Competition: The Debate competition held on 16th November, 2017 on the topic of “Post Demonetization Success or Failure?”

Book Exhibition: Book exhibition was organized with various book sections Management Sciences, Fiction, Novel, Technical Books etc. Students and faculty members attended the exhibition and recommended some of the books to the Library.

Blood Donation Camp

A “Blood Donation Camp” was organized by AUCBM in the campus in association with the INSTITUTE OF PREVENTIVE MEDICINE. Majority of the students have come forward voluntarily for donating the blood.

Independence Day

Seventy First Independence Day Celebrations was observed on 15th August, The program started with unfurls of National Flag and National Anthem was sung. Professors G.V.K. Reddy and N. Thirupalu delivered the speech on importance of Independence Day. Students, teaching and non-teaching staff have participated in the celebrations.

Teacher’s Day

Teacher’s Day was celebrated with great joy and splendor on 4th September,in the colleges. The students have presented a short video showing their appreciation towards teachers. It was a day of delight for the teachers to see the talents of their students exhibiting various performances. The students presented the gifts to all the teachers. The teachers enjoyed the day thoroughly and finally the day ended with a cake cutting ceremony. It was a thanksgiving event for the teachers from their students and the day ended on a happy note.

As a part of general club the college celebrates the birthdays of all the faculty members. On behalf of the college, the principal and the staff members will presents a gift along with a birthday greeting card as a token of love and affection to the person whose birthday is celebrated.

Potluck

AUCBM“Potluck” lunch was organized. All staff came together to enjoy an assortment of classic family recipes and homemade sweets, salads, starters, North and South Indian curries including vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes. Everyone relished the food and had a happy time bonding and sharing the food. No doubt it has strengthened the bonding among the staff members.
